Our Story

Since Establishment

The Art of Living.

ARTMILL INTERIORS was founded on the principle that a space should be more than just functional; it should be a reflection of the soul. Our journey began with a small team of passionate architects and designers who shared a vision for elevating the standard of living in Bahrain and beyond.

Today, we are recognized as a premier luxury design studio, known for our ability to blend contemporary minimal aesthetics with classic architectural foundations. Our portfolio spans from ultra-modern high-rise apartments to sprawling coastal villas, each treated with the same level of intimacy and detail.
